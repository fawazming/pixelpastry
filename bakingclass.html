10 Days at a Glance

01

General Introduction

- Personal introductions of the instructor and participants.
- Introduction to essential ingredients
- Advantages for stay-at-home moms

02

Doughnut Making - Introduction and Hands-on

- Introduction to the classic doughnut.
- Demonstration and hands-on experience.
- Learning different shaping and frying techniques.

03

Perfecting Doughnuts

- Detailed analysis of doughnut making challenges and how to overcome them.
- Individual Practical class

04

Puff Puff Making - Introduction

- Introduction to Puff Puff, a popular African snack.
- Hands-on practice in preparing Puff Puff batter and frying techniques

05

Perfecting Puff Puff

- Individual Practical class
- Analyzing common issues in Puff Puff making and troubleshooting solutions.

06

Bread Making - Introduction

- Introduction to bread making, exploring the magic of yeast and fermentation.
- Essential do's and don'ts in bread making for successful loaves.

07

Perfecting Bread Making

- Understanding the importance of proper yeast activation and fermentation times.
- Individual Practical class

08

Cake Baking - Introduction and Hands-on

- Introduction to cake baking, exploring different cake types and mixing methods.
- Do's and don'ts in cake baking for moist and tender cakes.

09

Meat Pie Baking - Introduction and Hands-on

- Introduction to savory baking with meat pies.
- Tips for creating flavorful fillings and achieving the perfect flaky crust.

10

Baking Presentation and Graduation

- Students showcase their final baked creations and discuss their experiences during the course.
- Certificate presentation to mark the successful completion of the course.
- Celebration of accomplishments and future baking aspirations.

Find out everything you need to know and what you will be learning
